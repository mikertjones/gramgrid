How to play

You are given 4 4-letter words and their letter values.

Each word is to be arranged in the 4 2x2 blocks in the 3x3 grid corners. NOTE: the words do not have to read properly clockwise or anti-clockwise.

What's important is that the total of the letter values must match the row and column targets. In the first shot the rows add up OK but the columns are incorrect

There is only ONE arrangement where the totals match the row and column targets - as seen in this shot.

Find the word made by the 9 letters when the grid is complete.
