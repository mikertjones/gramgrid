How to play

You are given 4 4-letter words and their letter values.

Each word is to be arranged in the 4 2x2 blocks in the 3x3 grid corners.

There is more than one way to arrange the words but only ONE way where the letter values add up to the row and column targets

Find the word made by the 9 letters when the grid is complete.
